The experience


I utilized two techniques to move the particles of the turtle: GPGPU and FLOW FIELD. GPGPU, or General-Purpose computing on Graphics Processing Units, leverages the GPU for data processing rather than pixel rendering, making it ideal for performing complex calculations repeatedly and efficiently. Meanwhile, the flow field technique employs spatialized streams to direct the particles' movement, creating dynamic and intricate patterns.
